4 Grilled Cheese Recipes

1 set of directions, 4 different sandwiches

  1. Stack sandwich ingredients and butter each pan facing piece of bread.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon salted butter in a cast-iron skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Press the sandwich slighty and place it in the skillet. Cook until golden on the bottom, 3-5 mins.
  4. Flip, adding more butter to the pan if needed, and cook until the other side is golden and the cheese melts, 3-5 mins.
